QUICK EMAIL SETUP GUIDE
=======================

To enable email with PDF ticket:

1. Go to: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
   (You must have 2-Step Verification enabled)

2. Create App Password:
   - App: Mail
   - Device: Other (Custom) → "Train Booking"
   - Click Generate
   - Copy the 16-character password

3. Edit backend/.env file and UNCOMMENT these lines:
   
   EMAIL_USER=your_email@gmail.com
   EMAIL_PASS=your_16_char_password
   
   Replace with your actual email and app password

4. Restart backend server:
   cd backend
   npm start

5. Test by creating a booking!

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- Check backend console for email logs
- Look for "✅ Email sent successfully" or "❌ Email sending failed"
- Check spam folder in your email
- Verify 2-Step Verification is enabled on Google Account
